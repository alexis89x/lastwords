
# May 19 - Muniz, Pedro

**Date of Birth**: September 25  

**Date of Offense**: December 20  

**Date of execution**: May 19  

**Summary of Incident**  
Muniz was sentenced to die for the December, 1976 Rape/Murder of 19-year-old Janis Carol Bickham, a student at Southwestern University in Georgetown.

**Last statement**  
I know you can't hear me now but I know that it won't matter what I have to say.  
I want you to know that I did not kill your sister. If you want to know the truth, and you deserve to know the truth, hire your own investigators.  
That's all I have to say.

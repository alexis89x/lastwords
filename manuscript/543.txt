
# July 27 - Preyor, Taichin

**Date of Birth**: September 02  

**Date of Offense**: February 26  

**Date of execution**: July 27  

**Summary of Incident**  
On 2/26/2004 in Bexar County, Texas, Preyor entered the apartment of his girlfriend, a twenty year old white female, and fatally stabbed her. As Preyor fled the scene, he allegedly stabbed an adult Hispanic male that was in the apartment.

**Last statement**  
First and foremost I'd like to say, "Justice has never advanced by taking a life" by Coretta Scott King. Lastly, to my wife and to my kids, I love y'all forever and always. That's it.

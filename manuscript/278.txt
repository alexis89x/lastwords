# August 20, 2002 - Etheridge, Gary

**Date of Birth**: 01/03/1964
**Date of Offense**: 02/02/1990
**Date of execution**: 08/20/2002

**Summary of Incident**
Convicted in the February 1990 murder of 15-year-old Christi Chauvierre. Chauvierre was sexually abused and stabbed to death when Etheridge went to her Brazoria County home looking for money to buy drugs. The victim's
mother, Gail Chauvierre, was also stabbed numerous times in the attack, but survived. Etheridge had once worked for Gail Chauvierre, who manages a condominium complex near Surfside.

**Last statement**
Yes, sir. To the victim's family, I'm sorry for what was taken from you. I hope you find peace. To my sweet Claudia, I love you. Stay strong, keep building, and be careful. Be careful. I love you. I'm through.

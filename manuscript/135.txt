# September 25, 1997 - Stone, Benjamin

**Date of Birth**: 06/03/1952
**Date of Offense**: 07/01/1995
**Date of execution**: 09/25/1997

**Summary of Incident**
Convicted in the strangling death of his 34-year-old ex-wife Patsy Lynn Stone and his 12-year-old step-daughter Keitha Lynn Van Coney at their Corpus Christi home. Stone used his hands to strangle the two victims following a verbal and physical altercation between himself and his ex-wife. The victims were also sexually assaulted by Stone. Stone was arrested the next day after he called 911 from a highway rest area and confessed to killing the two women.

**Last statement**
This offender declined to make a last statement.

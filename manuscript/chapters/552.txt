
## June 27 - Bible, Danny Paul

**Date of Birth**: August 28, 1951  

**Date of Offense**: May 27, 1979  

**Date of execution**: June 27, 2018  

**Summary of Incident**  
On 05/27/1979, in Harris County, Bible attacked and sexually assaulted a white female that came to his residence to use the phone. Bible then stabbed the victim multiple times, resulting in her death.

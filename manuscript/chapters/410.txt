
## August 05 - Medellin, Jose

**Date of Birth**: March 04, 1975  

**Date of Offense**: June 24, 1993  

**Date of execution**: August 05, 2008  

**Summary of Incident**  
Convicted in the kidnapping, rape and strangulation of 16-year-old Elizabeth Pena. Pena and Jennifer Ertman 15, were attacked by Medellin and five other members of the Black & White gang as they walked home near T.C. Jester Park in Houston. After sexually assaulting Pena, Medellin reportedly strangled her because he did not want to leave witnesses.

**Last statement**  
I am sorry my actions caused pain. I hope this brings closure to what you seek. Don't ever hate them for what they do. Never harbor hate. I love you. Alright Warden.

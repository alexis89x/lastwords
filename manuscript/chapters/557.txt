

## December 04 - Garcia, Joseph Christopher

**Date of Birth**: June 11, 1971  

**Date of Offense**: December 24, 2000  

**Date of execution**: December 04, 2018  

**Summary of Incident**  
On 12/24/2000, in Irving, Texas, Garcia and six codefendants fatally shot a 31 year old white male police officer while on escape from the TDCJ Connally Unit.

**Last statement**  
Yes Sir. Dear Heavenly Father please forgive them for they know not what they do.


## August 20 - Kelly, Carl

**Date of Birth**: March 27, 1959  

**Date of Offense**: September 01, 1980  

**Date of execution**: August 20, 1993  

**Summary of Incident**  
Kelly was convicted of capital murder for his role in the September, 1980 overnight robbery, abduction and murder spree in Waco. Victims were: Steven Pryor (convenience store clerk) and David Wade Riley (transient). Kelly allegedly shot both men then threw their bodies over a cliff.

**Last statement**  
I'm an African warrior, born to breathe, and born to die.

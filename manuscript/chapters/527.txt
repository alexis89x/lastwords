

## June 18 - Russeau, Gregory

**Date of Birth**: October 11, 1969  

**Date of Offense**: May 30, 2001  

**Date of execution**: June 18, 2015  

**Summary of Incident**  
On 05/30/2001, in Tyler, Russeau struck a 75-year old white male numerous times in the head causing his death. Russeau also took the victim's wallet and a vehicle from the victim's place of business.

**Last statement**  
'Yes, I would like to thank my family and friends for what y'all have done for me.' Thank you for being here with me that I do not have to transition alone.' I have peace.' To my daughter, I love you, to my grandbabies, sisters and brothers, I love you.' I am ready to go home.'

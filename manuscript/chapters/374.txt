
## August 24 - Fuller, Justin

**Date of Birth**: August 29, 1978  

**Date of Offense**: April 21, 1997  

**Date of execution**: August 24, 2006  

**Summary of Incident**  
On April 21, 1997, Fuller abducted, robbed, and murdered a 22 year old white male near Tyler. The victim was bound and blindfolded inside his Tyler apartment and driven to his bank where $300 was withdrawn from his account using his ATM card. The victim was then driven to Lake Tyler and shot three times with a .22-caliber weapon.

**Last statement**  
Yes I do, I would like to tell my family thank you for your support, and my friends. And let everyone know that you must stay strong for each other. Take care of yourselves. That's it, Warden.

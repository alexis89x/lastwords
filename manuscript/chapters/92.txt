
## February 21 - Hawkins, Samuel

**Date of Birth**: September 01, 1943  

**Date of Offense**: February 03, 1976  

**Date of execution**: February 21, 1995  

**Summary of Incident**  
Sentenced to death for the slaying of Abbe Rogus Hamilton, 19, of Borger. She was six months pregnant when she was allegedly raped and stabbed to death with a hunting knife.

**Last statement**  
This offender declined to make a last statement.

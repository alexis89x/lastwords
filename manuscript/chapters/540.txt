

## January 26 - Edwards, Terry

**Date of Birth**: August 10, 1973  

**Date of Offense**: July 08, 2002  

**Date of execution**: January 26, 2017  

**Summary of Incident**  
On July 8, 2002 in Dallas, Edwards and co-defendent Kirk Edwards entered a restaurant, fatally shot an adult male and an adult female, took money from the cash register and fled the scene.

**Last statement**  
Yes, I made peace with God. I hope y'all make peace with this.


## October 05 - Fuller, Barney Ronald Jr.

**Date of Birth**: August 12, 1958  

**Date of Offense**: May 14, 2003  

**Date of execution**: October 05, 2016  

**Summary of Incident**  
On 05/14/2003 in Houston County, Texas, Fuller entered the residence of his neighbors, a forty-three year old white male and a thirty-nine year old white female and fatally shot both of them multiple times. Fuller then and fled the scene on foot.

**Last statement**  
I don't have anything to say, you can proceed Warden Jones.

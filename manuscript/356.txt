
# January 25, 2006 - Dudley, Marion

**Date of Birth**: May 13, 1972  

**Date of Offense**: June 20, 1992  

**Date of execution**: January 25, 2006  

**Summary of Incident**  
Convicted in the murders of three people shot during a drug buy at a Houston residence. Killed were Jose Tovar, Jessica Quinones and Frank Farias. Dudley and two accomplices had gone to the home of Jose and Rachel Tovar to buy three kilograms of cocaine when they decided to rob them of their drugs and money. Three other  
people were also shot by the conspirators, two of which survived head wounds to identify Dudley as one of their attackers.

**Last statement**  
This offender declined to make a last statement.

# 2000-11-09 - Flores, Miguel

**Date of Birth**: 1969-06-07  

**Date of Offense**: 1989-06-28  

**Date of execution**: 2000-11-09  

**Summary of Incident**  
Convicted of capital murder for kidnapping, sexually assaulting, and murdering a 20 year old white female. Flores kidnapped the victim from a video store and took her to a remote area where he sexually assaulted her. Flores drove the victim back to town in her car, eventually stabbing her to death. Flores left the victim in her car and fled the scene.

**Last statement**  
I want to thank my attorneys, Father Walsh...Sylvia, te quiero mucho y a Consulado, te quiero decir muchas gracias por todo. I want to say I am sorry and I say a prayer today for you so you can have peace and I hope that you can forgive me. God is waiting and God is waiting now.

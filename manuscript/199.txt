# 1999-12-15 - Felder, Jr., Sammie

**Date of Birth**: 1945-08-23  

**Date of Offense**: 1975-02-26  

**Date of execution**: 1999-12-15  

**Summary of Incident**  
Convicted in the February 1975 stabbing death of 42-year-old James Hanks, a paraplegic living at the Independent Life Style Apartment Complex in Houston. Hanks was reportedly stabbed with a pair of scissors  
and money taken from his apartment in the 5100 block of South Willow. Felder was working as an attendant at the complex at the time of the murder. He was arrested by Idaho police for a traffic violation a month after the killing.

**Last statement**  
Like to tell my friends that I love them. Appreciate them being here to support me. Alison, I love you.

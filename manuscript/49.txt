# May 20, 1992 - Romero, Jr., Jesus

**Date of Birth**: 02/03/1965
**Date of Offense**: 12/24/1984
**Date of execution**: 05/20/1992

**Summary of Incident**
Convicted in the December 1984 rape-slaying of 15 year old Olga Perales near San Benito. Romero and three co-defendants drove Perales to a remote location 1.3 miles west of State Highway 2520 where she was raped repeatedly and then beaten around the head with a pipe and stabbed twice in the chest with a knife. Her nude body was found the same day in a brushy area off Kilgore Road.

**Last statement**
When his attorney came into the witness room, he said, "Tell Mom I love her." The attorney said back to him, "I love you, too."

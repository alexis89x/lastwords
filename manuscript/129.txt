# 1997-06-16 - Stoker, David

**Date of Birth**: 1959-01-25  

**Date of Offense**: 1986-11-07  

**Date of execution**: 1997-06-16  

**Summary of Incident**  
Convicted in the November 1986 robbery-slaying of 50-year-old David Manrrique, a clerk at Allsup's convenience store in Hale Center. Manrrique was shot with a .22-caliber pistol during an early morning robbery that netted $60. Stoker is also serving a 30-year sentence for delivery of methamphetamine. That sentence was assessed in Swisher Co. in August 1988.

**Last statement**  
I have a statement prepared that I have given to the Chaplain that I want released to the media.  
I am ready, Warden.

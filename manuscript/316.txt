# 2004-01-21 - Zimmerman, Kevin

**Date of Birth**: 1961-05-17  

**Date of Offense**: 1987-10-23  

**Date of execution**: 2004-01-21  

**Summary of Incident**  
Convicted in the October 1987 stabbing death of 33-year-old Leslie Gilbert Hooks, Jr. at a Beaumont motel. Hooks, a resident of Suisan City, California, was stabbed with a knife inside a room of the Motel 6 at 2640 IH 10. His body was found the next morning by a maid. Evidence, including the murder weapon, was later located and linked to Zimmerman. He was arrested on Oct. 28, 1987.

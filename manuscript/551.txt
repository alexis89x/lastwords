
# June 16 - Castillo, Juan Edward

**Date of Birth**: April 03  

**Date of Offense**: December 03  

**Date of execution**: June 16  

**Summary of Incident**  
On 12/03/2003 in Bexar County, Castillo and 3 co-defendants fatally shot a 19 year old Hispanic male after demanding his money.

**Last statement**  
To everyone that has been there for me you know who you are. Love y'all. See y'all on the other side.  
That's it.

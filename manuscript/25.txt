
# July 08, 1987 - Thompson, John

**Date of Birth**: January 27, 1955  

**Date of Offense**: May 10, 1977  

**Date of execution**: July 08, 1987  

**Summary of Incident**  
Thompson was convicted of capital murder in the May 1977 shooting death of 70-year-old Mary Kneupper during a robbery attempt at her mini-storage business in San Antonio. Kneupper was shot in the neck with a .45-caliber pistol and later died at a San Antonio hospital.

**Last statement**  
This offender declined to make a last statement.
